### Spread operator
The Spread Operator `expands` an `array` into its `elements`. It can also be used for object literals.

#### Example:
```js
const favouriteFood = [ 'Pizza', 'Fries', 'Sandwitch' ];
console.log(...favouriteFood);
//Pizza Fries Sandwitch
```
